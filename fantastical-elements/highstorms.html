Highstorms

Highstorms are a catastrophic natural phenomenon, characterized by their devastating impact on both the physical and metaphysical realms. Unlike conventional storms, Highstorms manifest with disconcerting moments of darkness instead of lightning flashes and emit high-pitched, eerie sounds rather than deep, rolling thunder.

Origins

Highstorms originated from the extensive and unrestrained use of weather manipulation magic by mages in a period predating the Era of Legends and the adoption of the Druidic Calendar. This form of magic, wielded to shape the world's climate, led to awe-inspiring changes but also had dangerous, unforeseen consequences. The Highstorms were an unintended side effect of this manipulation, disrupting the natural order and posing a severe threat to all known life.

Characteristics

  • Dark Flashes: Instead of traditional lightning, Highstorms produce moments of darkness that tear through the atmosphere.
  • Eerie Sounds: The storms are accompanied by high-pitched, unsettling screeches, replacing the typical sound of thunder.
  • Destructive Force: Highstorms possess the power to disrupt both the physical world and the fabric of reality itself, making them a dire threat to the known world.

Conflict and Impact on Magic

The emergence of Highstorms led to a schism among magic users. While many mages continued their pursuits, oblivious or indifferent to the consequences, a faction known as the Druids recognized the gravity of the situation. This division culminated in brutal magical wars, with casualties too numerous to count. Ultimately, the Druids emerged victorious, determined to rectify the mistakes of the past and banish the Highstorms.

The Druidic Sacrifice

The Druidic Sacrifice marks the most pivotal and solemn event in the annals of the known world, signaling the end of the Era of Storms. Faced with the catastrophic impact of the Highstorms, the Druid Circles united in a secretive, large-scale ritual aimed at banishing the storms beyond the borders of the known world. This effort, however, came at an extraordinary cost: every Druid involved sacrificed their life, marking the ritual with a profound loss.
The Druidic Sacrifice is commemorated as the official beginning of the Druidic Calendar and is a crucial part of Druidic heritage. It serves as a solemn reminder of the dangers of unchecked magical manipulation and the importance of preserving the natural order. Today, the manipulation of weather is outlawed across the world, sometimes even under penalty of death, as societies seek to prevent the recurrence of such cataclysms.

Religion and Spirituality

The nature of Highstorms and how they came about, led some people to believe there to be a higher power responsible for them, one who did not like how much magic users meddled with their affairs. They founded the Order of the Storms, a dark cult that tries to get rid of all magic users.

Beyond the Stormwall

What lies beyond the Highstorms remains one of the greatest mysteries of the known world. The storms serve as a barrier, encapsulating the known world and keeping its inhabitants from discovering what lies beyond. Despite this, the Druids and other custodians of the world's natural order continue their vigil, ensuring that the Highstorms remain at bay and that the balance of the world is maintained.
